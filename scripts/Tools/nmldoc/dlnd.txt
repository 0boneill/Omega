 
    =========================================
    DLND naming conventions
    =========================================
  
             _DLND%SCPL : dlnd modes are DLND_MODE=GLC_CPLHIST:
             _DLND%LCPL : dlnd modes are DLND_MODE=CPLHIST:
             _DLND%NULL : dlnd modes are DLND_MODE=NULL:
